We're the Pi Club!
Explore! Build! Change!

Pi Club is a student group that constantly tries to innovate and foster technical activitites in the campus. Since 2018, we have focused on creating meaningful experiences through immersive learning. We hope to bring the best in code, development and design to the college scene.

About Us

Pi Club is a community of students highly enthusiastic about coding, Designing and all other aspects of programming. We are a team currently of 12 members . We organize activities related to Development (Web Development, Open Source, Game/App Development), programming contests, hackathons etc. Lecture series are regularly taken on algorithms, web hijacking, development, among other things. Apart from this, we also develop various applications to be used by the campus junta; like Mess Managment System promote Open Source. Feel free to contact any of our members any time in case of any queries! We hang out on Lab at CA Department Happy Coding!

Opportunities Pi Club provide students with

  • Grow their knowledge on developer technologies and more through peer to peer workshops and events.
  • Gain relevant industry experience by solving problems for local organizations with technology based solutions.
  • Showcase their prototypes and solutions to their local community and industry leaders.
  • Getting inspiration to become world-class developers and changemakers from sharing others' success stories.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can become a member?

The club is open to any student, ranging from novice developers who are just starting, to advanced developers who want to further their skills.

How frequently do events and workshops occur?

We often have hands-on session every Saturday and Sunday . We also hold workshops and showcases & we would recommend you to join our community on Facebook to get updates.

What should I carry when attending a workshop?

We recommend you carry with you a notebook, pen and a laptop because more often than not we get our hands dirty with code. Most importantly, bring along a healthy dose of curiosity and enthusiasm.

Who should I reach out to if I have any questions?

If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact Feel free to contact any of our members any time . We hang out on Lab at Computer Application Department .
